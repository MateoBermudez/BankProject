About UniBank

Your trusted financial partner on campus.

Our Mission

At UniBank, our mission is to provide university students and staff with accessible, affordable, and user-friendly banking services that support their financial well-being throughout their academic journey and beyond.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, UniBank was created by a group of university students who recognized the need for banking services that truly understood and addressed the unique financial challenges faced by students.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Making banking services available to all students regardless of their financial background.
  • Education: Empowering students with financial knowledge and tools.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our services to meet evolving student needs.
  • Community: Building a supportive financial community within the university ecosystem.
